<LEFT> <SIZE:small> Whether you're strolling through the town square, browsing stores, or just chatting with friendly locals - West Point is true Small Town USA. <LINE> <LINE>
Don't let sleepy streets, affordable housing and acres of rolling farmland fool you: this is a Knox Country administrative hub. If our Mayor isn't busy rubber-stamping public ordinances in the Town Hall, you might just find him in the famous Twiggy's Bar! No matter how long your stay, a West Point welcome is guaranteed.
